About AIPlatformDataSecurity

Empowering IT Security Professionals Since 2019

Our Mission

To create a global community of IT security professionals who are committed to continuous learning, constructive criticism, and professional growth. We believe that honest feedback and rigorous learning standards are essential for developing world-class security expertise.

Our Vision

To become the premier platform for IT security professionals who want to challenge themselves, learn from peers worldwide, and develop the thick skin necessary to thrive in the demanding field of cybersecurity.

Our History

2019

The Beginning

AIPlatformDataSecurity was founded as a WhatsApp group for IT professionals seeking serious, no-nonsense learning opportunities in cybersecurity.

2020

Global Expansion

The group expanded internationally, attracting members from different continents and diverse technical backgrounds.

2021

Structured Learning

Introduced formal learning schedules and accountability measures to ensure consistent professional development.

2022

Community Growth

Reached significant milestones in member engagement and successful career transitions within the community.

2023

Digital Presence

Launched YouTube channel and expanded online presence to reach more security professionals.

2024

Official Website

Launched our official website to better serve our community and provide resources for members.
